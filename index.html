Each piece of jewelry is individually hand-crafted from precious metals, stones, and pearls, created specifically for your enjoyment.

COLLECTIONS

Wave Series

Living in a coastal town in Maine, I draw my inspiration for the Wave Series from the rhythm of the ocean. Using the forging technique, I have the opportunity to form the metal in a variety of shapes and thicknesses creating a fluid, tailored, classic style.

Maine Charms

People come to Maine to enjoy nature and its beauty, experience unique wilderness, and relax on picturesque shores, creating memories that last a life time. The Maine Charm Bracelet was designed to capture the splendor of Maine.

Heart Collection

The appealing shape and the symbolism of the heart has captured people’s attention for many years. I incorporate the heart in many of my designs and hope my jewelry is an expression of the excitement I feel for life and the experience of living. This timeless classic line of jewelry can be used to celebrate any occasion that expresses love and sincerity.

ABOUT

My fascination in creating jewelry is inspired by working with precious metals, stones, and pearls. As an artist, I enjoy creating connections with my customers by providing them with pieces that represent an expression of love, a memory, a celebration, one of life’s experiences, or a visit to our beautiful state of Maine.
